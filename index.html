This is a visualisation of the regulatory network of B. subtilis. There are two network sources: literature and predictions by a kinetic model. You can switch between those on the top left.

Legend:

  • Red nodes are sigma factors
  • Yellow edges represent unspecified regulation (in the literature network)
  • Green edges represent positive regulations, we have more confidence in dark green edges than in light green
  • Blue edges represent negative regulations, we have more confidence in dark blue edges than light blue
